# Quick vegan recipes

Hagar's "the microwave is your friend" collection — fast, forgiving, fully plant-based. Nothing is mandatory; season to taste. Pull these into menus when the user wants something quick.

## Basics

**Microwave rice** — 1 cup basmati rice, 2 cups boiling water, salt or vegan bouillon (start with ½ tsp; aim a touch saltier than seems right), 1 tsp olive oil. In a *deep* Pyrex bowl, stir rice with salt and oil to coat, add the boiling water, cover loosely, microwave 15 min. (Use a deep bowl — it boils over easily.)

**Microwave pasta** — 1 cup dry pasta, 2 cups boiling water, a little salt. Deep covered Pyrex, 5 min; if still firm, add 2–3 min.

**Microwave vegetables** — green/yellow beans, broccoli, cauliflower, beet, zucchini, carrot, pepper, fennel (frozen is fine). Covered Pyrex ~10 min; remove carefully, stir, add 5–10 min. Seasoning ideas: green beans with soy sauce + sesame oil · broccoli & cauliflower with salt + garlic · zucchini & carrot with bouillon (or salt) + dill · peppers with a little vinegar, garlic, salt.

## Spreads & soups

**Orange vegetable spread** (sandwiches or sauce) — 2 large carrots + 1 large red pepper, rough-chopped; 1–2 Tbsp vinegar or lemon juice; 1 garlic clove; salt; chili if you like; optional 1 tsp olive oil. Microwave the carrots and pepper 5–7 min until soft (covered Pyrex), add seasonings, blend with an immersion blender, adjust.

**Romani eggplant spread** — 1 large eggplant (cubed), 1 large red pepper, 1–2 Tbsp vinegar, 1 garlic clove, ½ tsp dried oregano (optional), salt, optional 1 tsp olive oil. Microwave 5–7 min until soft, season, blend. (Far better with roasted vegetables.) Same method works for cauliflower, fennel, beet, or squash spreads.

**Zucchini cream soup** — 2 zucchini (cubed), 1 garlic clove, ½–1 tsp vegan chicken-style bouillon, 1 tsp dried dill (or 1 Tbsp fresh), black pepper, salt if needed. Microwave ~10 min until soft, season, blend. Thin with a little hot water to taste.

**Lazy miso soup** — 1 Tbsp miso paste thinned gradually with hot water (like loosening tahini); ½ block tofu, cubed; 3 Tbsp dried wakame; 2 green onions, chopped; 2 dried shiitake, crumbled by hand. Add ~1½ cups boiling water and stir.

## Proteins & "eggs"

**Fast legume patties** — ⅔ cup (100 g) chickpea or lentil flour, 1 tsp bouillon, ¼ tsp baking powder, 2 Tbsp nutritional yeast (optional), garlic granules. Stir in ¼ cup water to a batter (+1 Tbsp if needed), mix in 1 small chopped onion (or pepper/mushrooms). Drop heaping spoonfuls on a greased plate; 4 min in the microwave.

**Baked or grilled tofu** — 1 block tofu, sliced ~1 cm. Spread both sides with prepared BBQ sauce; sear on a grill pan (1 tsp oil) or bake on parchment at 180°C ~20 min (longer for more char). Better if marinated a few hours first.
*Tastier version:* slice 1 block tofu; whisk ⅓ cup soy sauce, 2 Tbsp silan (date syrup) or 4 Tbsp BBQ sauce, 1–2 cloves garlic (or 1 tsp garlic granules), 2 tsp olive oil; bake or sear.

**Tofu "egg" salad / scramble** — ½ block tofu, grated or fork-mashed. Must-haves: black salt (kala namak — careful, salty and sulfurous) and turmeric. Add 3–4 Tbsp water or unsweetened soy milk to bind; mix well. Optional: garlic granules, nutritional yeast, black pepper, green onion. Eat cold or warm; pour over ready tomato sauce for shakshuka, or fold in avocado for an egg-avocado salad.

**Chickpea/lentil flour omelet** — ⅓ cup (3 Tbsp) lentil or chickpea flour + ⅓ cup water or plant milk, whisked to an egg-like batter (add water if needed). Optional: black salt (egg flavor), turmeric, black pepper, garlic granules, herbs, mushrooms, green onion. Fry in 1 tsp oil in a good non-stick pan until dry on top, then flip.

**Tofu "cheese"** — 150 g tofu (½ block), 1 Tbsp olive oil (optional), 1–3 Tbsp lemon juice, 1 garlic clove, 1 Tbsp nutritional yeast (recommended), salt, water. Blend, adding water gradually to the texture you like; taste and adjust. Flavor options: garlic-dill, pesto, sun-dried tomato, roasted pepper, olives.

**Red lentil spread** — ½ kg red lentils (soak 2–3 hrs if you can; otherwise add more water and cook longer). To taste: 3 Tbsp raw whole-sesame tahini (more if you like), 1 tsp salt, juice of 1 lemon, 2 cloves garlic crushed; optional sweet paprika (for color) and cumin. Cook the lentils until they fall apart, take off the heat, add the rest, blend smooth (or leave grainy), adjust after it cools.

## Extras & baking

**Healthy chocolate balls** — 1 heaping Tbsp ground flaxseed, 1 tsp cocoa, 1 tsp silan (or 1 tsp sugar + 2 tsp water), a touch of vanilla. Mix to a paste, roll into 1–2 balls, roll in coconut.

**3-ingredient breakfast cookies** — mash 2 ripe bananas (applesauce works), 1½ cups fine oats, ⅓ cup add-in (raisins / cranberries / chocolate chips); optional cinnamon or vanilla. Form small flat cookies; bake on parchment ~12–15 min at 180°C.

**Microwave lentil/chickpea bun** (~under 200 kcal, ~7–8 g protein) — 3 Tbsp lentil/chickpea flour, ¼ tsp baking powder, 2 tsp plain soy yogurt or soy cheese (or applesauce / mashed banana), 1 tsp olive oil, 1 Tbsp + 1 tsp plant milk, a little salt. Mix; 1 min in the microwave (may need another 10–30 sec).

**Vegan buttermilk** — 1 cup unsweetened soy milk + 1–2 Tbsp lemon juice; stir.
